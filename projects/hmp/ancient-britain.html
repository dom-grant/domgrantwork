Ancient Britain for Modern Folk

2025
Cover Design, Editorial Design, Production Design.
Published by Hoxton Mini Press.
Written by Tom Howells, edited by Florence Ward.

Ancient Britian for Modern Folk is a pithy reference manual, exploring the histories and mysteris of over 90 ancient sites, from the colossal stones of Avebury to the remains of Neolithic villages in Orkney.
As a pocketable handbook, Ancient Britain for Modern Folk evokes the design of 1920s field guides.
